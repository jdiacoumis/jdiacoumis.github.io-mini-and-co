Classes

What happens in a Mini & Co. class.

A 45-minute session of gentle sensory play, songs, and unhurried connection — held twice each Wednesday in a space designed to feel calm from the moment you walk in.

The class flow

Gentle from start to finish.

Each class follows the same gentle rhythm — predictable enough that babies (and mums) know what to expect, with enough room for each little person to set their own pace.

  • Welcome & settle. Soft music, a chance to find your mat, and a hello song to ease everyone in.
  • Sensory invitation. A simple, age-appropriate play setup — open-ended materials, no pressure, no overstimulation.
  • Connection moments. Songs, mirroring, and prompts that turn ordinary play into "serve and return" interaction.
  • Free exploration. Time for babies to follow their curiosity, while mums chat or simply rest.
  • Gentle close. A familiar goodbye song, and a calm transition out the door.
Who it's for

Babies 3–12 months and their mums.

If your baby cries, clings, feeds, naps, or simply watches — that's all welcome here. There's no "doing it right." The class is designed to flex around your baby's mood and stage.

Classes are kept small so the room stays quiet, the connection stays meaningful, and no one feels lost in the shuffle.

What to bring

Just yourself and your baby.

Mats, sensory materials, props, and music are all set up and ready. We've thought about the small things so you don't have to.

  • Comfortable clothes you can sit on the floor in
  • A water bottle, if you'd like one
  • A muslin or burp cloth — whatever your baby usually needs
  • A pram parking spot is available just outside the room
Where

Sandown Room, Oran Park Library.

The Sandown Room is a quiet, naturally lit space inside Oran Park Library — gentle on babies' senses, accessible, and easy to find.

Full address details and what to look for on arrival are sent to everyone who registers.
